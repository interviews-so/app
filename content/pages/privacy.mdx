## Introduction

This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our technical interview scenarios product ("Product"). By accessing or using the Product, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

1. **User Data:** We may collect personal information such as name, email address, and company details when you register or use our Product.

2. **Usage Information:** We collect information on how you interact with the Product, including access times, features used, and any feedback provided.

## Use of Information

1. **Operational Use:** We use the information collected to operate and maintain the Product, ensuring a high-quality user experience.

2. **Communication:** We may use your contact information to send important updates, notifications, or respond to inquiries.

## Data Security

1. **Protection:** Your data is stored securely on our systems. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of your information.

2. **No Sale of Data:** We do not sell or expose any customer data to third parties.

## User Control

1. **Data Deletion:** You may request the deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at account-deletion@interviews.so. Upon receiving such a request, we will promptly remove your information from our systems.

2. **Opt-Out:** You have the option to opt-out of receiving non-essential communications from us.

## Compliance with Laws

We will comply with all applicable laws and regulations governing the privacy of your information.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

Interviews.so reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of any material changes.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@interviews.so.
